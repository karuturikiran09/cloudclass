## 💡 KEY FEATURES EXPLAINED

### 1. 📹 Video Calls
- **Platforms**: Jitsi (free!), Google Meet, Zoom, Teams
- **Auto-Links**: Jitsi links generated automatically
- **No Account**: Jitsi works without registration
- **Schedule**: Faculty schedule, students join
- **One-Click**: Join calls with single click

### 2. 💬 Chat System
- **Real-Time**: Updates every 5 seconds
- **Course-Based**: Separate chat per course
- **Unread Counter**: Shows unread messages
- **Delete**: Users can delete own messages
- **Faculty Badge**: Identifies instructors

### 3. 🔍 Google Search
- **Integrated**: Search bar in navbar
- **Two Methods**: Press Enter or click button
- **New Tab**: Opens results in new tab
- **Auto-Clear**: Input clears after search

### 4. 📅 Calendar View
- **Visual Timeline**: See all due dates
- **Color-Coded**: Overdue, due soon, upcoming
- **Sorted**: Chronological order
- **Days Counter**: Shows days until due

### 5. 👥 Study Groups
- **Create Groups**: Students form study groups
- **Join/Leave**: Easy group management
- **Meeting Links**: Integrate video calls
- **Member Limit**: Set maximum members

### 6. 📊 Analytics
- **Statistics**: Total students, assignments, grades
- **Submission Rate**: Track completion
- **Grade Distribution**: A/B/C/D/F breakdown
- **Visual Charts**: Progress bars and graphs

### 7. 🔔 Notifications
- **Bell Icon**: Shows unread count
- **Dropdown**: View all notifications
- **Mark Read**: Click to mark as read
- **Types**: Assignments, grades, announcements, etc.
